Who we are

Coffee, craft, and a place to belong.

Catapult is a social enterprise of Outflow Ministry, built for people who face real barriers to joining the workforce. Here, employment is mentorship — every shift is a chance to build the skills that open doors, served alongside a genuinely great cup of coffee.

We're committed to the dignity of everyone who walks through our door, and we hope you feel that in the craftsmanship of the space, the care in your cup, and the welcome at the counter.
